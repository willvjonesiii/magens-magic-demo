My Healing Journey

The road to
soul-deep healing

"What I know is that transformation is possible. Because I lived it."

My journey into Reiki and energy healing began long before I knew what Reiki was. Since the age of 15, I struggled with depression and anxiety. Over the years, I was prescribed numerous medications and received several mental health diagnoses, including bipolar disorder and later schizophrenia. I was hospitalized more times than I care to count, placed on disability, and eventually became almost completely bedridden — taking 14 different medications. And still, I did not feel better.

That search led me to my very first Reiki session, and something shifted in me that I had never felt before. Over time, in a turn that stunned even my own family, I was able to discontinue every medication I once believed I would need for the rest of my life.
